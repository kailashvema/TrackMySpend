Welcome to TrackMySpend

This web app helps you monitor your daily, weekly, or monthly expenses, set reminders, and understand your spending habits through clean visualizations.

Key Features

  • 💰 Record daily expenses
  • 📊 Visualize spending patterns
  • ⏰ Get payment reminders
  • 📁 Categorize income and expenses
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Share expenses with friends or family
